About Martingale

Martingale is a paper trading platform designed for educational purposes, providing a realistic simulation of financial markets without the risk of actual monetary loss.

What is Paper Trading?

Paper trading, also known as virtual trading or simulated trading, allows users to practice trading strategies using simulated money in real-time market conditions. It's an essential tool for learning how financial markets work and developing trading skills.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Price Simulation: Live market data simulation with realistic price movements
  • Portfolio Management: Track your virtual holdings, cash balance, and performance
  • Trading History: Complete transaction logs and time & sales data
  • Interactive Charts: Multiple chart views with technical analysis capabilities
  • Multi-User Support: Individual user accounts with persistent portfolios
  • Risk-Free Learning: Practice trading strategies without financial consequences

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies including Flask (Python web framework), Socket.IO (real-time communication), Chart.js (data visualization), and a custom terminal-inspired UI design.

Educational Purpose

This platform is specifically designed for educational use, helping users understand:

  • How financial markets operate
  • Portfolio management principles
  • Risk assessment and trading psychology
  • Technical analysis and chart reading
  • The importance of diversification

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

This is a paper trading platform for educational purposes only. All prices are simulated and do not reflect real market values. No actual money or securities are involved in any transactions.

This platform should not be used as the sole basis for making real investment decisions. Always consult with qualified financial professionals before making actual investments.
